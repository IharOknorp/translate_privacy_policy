# Privacy Policy — FileShift

Effective date: March 10, 2026

## 1. Who We Are

FileShift is a mobile application for offline file conversion on your device.

For privacy requests, contact: oknorpcorp@gmail.com

## 2. What Data We Process

Depending on how you use the app, we may process:

- Files you select for conversion and files generated by the app, stored locally on your device.
- Local app data such as conversion history, settings, consent choices, and app preferences.
- Advertising and consent-related data, including ad identifiers and consent signals where applicable.
- Purchase and subscription status through RevenueCat and store providers.
- Technical and diagnostic data such as app version, device type, crash data, and service logs.

We do not sell your personal data.

## 3. Why We Process Data

We process data to:

- provide file conversion features;
- store converted files and local history on your device;
- manage ads and subscription entitlements;
- maintain reliability, security, and abuse prevention;
- comply with legal obligations and respond to privacy requests.

## 4. Legal Bases

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, we rely on:

- contract, to provide requested file conversion functionality;
- consent, for ads, privacy choices, and optional analytics where required;
- legitimate interests, for security, abuse prevention, and service reliability;
- legal obligations, where we must comply with law or regulatory requests.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party providers, including:

- Google AdMob
- Google Firebase
- RevenueCat
- Apple App Store / Google Play billing providers

These providers may process data under their own privacy policies.

## 6. Storage and Retention

Selected files, converted files, and conversion history are stored locally on your device until you delete them or use the in-app deletion tools.

Third-party providers may retain operational logs, ad records, purchase metadata, and diagnostics according to their own retention policies.

## 7. International Transfers

Third-party providers may process data in countries other than your own, including countries with different data protection laws.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, withdrawal of consent, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

## 9. Children’s Privacy

FileShift is not directed to children below the age required by local law without parental or guardian consent.

## 10. Data Deletion

You can remove local app data directly in the app using the data deletion tools, or by uninstalling the app.

For privacy requests, email oknorpcorp@gmail.com with:

- your platform (Android/iOS),
- approximate date of last use,
- a short description of the request.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest effective date will appear at the top.
