## 1. Who We Are
AI Translator is a mobile application for text translation and related features (history, rewarded ads, subscription).

## 2. What Data We Process
Depending on your use of the App, we may process:

- **Translation input and output text** (to provide translation).
- **App analytics data** (events such as screen views, actions in the app).
- **Advertising-related data** (for AdMob rewarded ads, including ad identifiers where applicable).
- **Purchase/subscription status** (via RevenueCat and store providers).
- **Technical data** (device/app version, crash and diagnostics info).
- **Local app data** stored on your device (e.g., translation history, quota counters, promo usage flags).

We do **not** sell your personal data.

## 3. Why We Process Data
We process data to:

- provide translation functionality;
- maintain and improve app performance;
- measure feature usage and product quality;
- display and manage ads;
- manage subscriptions and entitlements;
- prevent abuse/fraud and ensure service reliability.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App may use third-party providers, including:

- **Google Firebase** (analytics, backend functions);
- **Google AdMob** (ads);
- **RevenueCat** (subscription management);
- **OpenAI API** (translation processing via backend function).

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

## 5. Data Storage and Retention
- Some data is stored **locally on your device** (e.g., translation history).
- Backend/service providers may store operational logs and service data for limited periods required for security and service operation.
- We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy.

## 6. Your Rights
Depending on your region, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection regarding your personal data.

## 7. Children’s Privacy
The App is not intended for children under the age required by local law without parental/guardian consent. If you believe a child has provided data improperly, contact us for deletion.

## 8. Data Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 9. International Transfers
Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including countries where our providers operate.

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” at the top indicates the latest version.

## 12. Data Deletion
If you want your data deleted, use one of the methods below.

### A) Delete local app data (on your device)
- Clear app data in device settings, or
- Reinstall the app.

This removes local history and local counters stored on the device.

### B) Request deletion from us
Send an email to **support@freeok.online** with subject:  
**“Data Deletion Request — AI Translator”**

Please include:
- platform (Android/iOS),
- approximate date of last app use,
- description of what you want deleted.

We will review and process deletion requests within a reasonable time and as required by applicable law.
